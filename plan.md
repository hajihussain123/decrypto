- one server, two players
- server fetches words and displays their words on their screens
- and also generates 3 digit number to write the hints
- gives chances to players to write hints and guess the hints
- whoever gets points by guessing is counted and whoever gets two points wins

 - and then when its time for guessing code it will display the whole board
